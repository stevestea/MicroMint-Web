The hero ingredient

Meet nano-hydroxyapatite

Roughly 97% of your tooth enamel is hydroxyapatite — a naturally occurring calcium mineral. Over time, acids and stains create micro-roughness on the surface that traps color from coffee, tea, and wine.

Nano-hydroxyapatite is a microscopic version that fills those tiny gaps, smooths the surface, and reflects light — so teeth look whiter while enamel is strengthened, not stripped.

  • Remineralizes & smooths enamel
  • Reflects light for a brighter look
  • No peroxide — so no sensitivity
